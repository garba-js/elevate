Our Challenges

Côte d'Ivoire faces high unemployment (36%) and persistent poverty (39.5%), impacting living conditions and access to education. Although poverty has improved since 2015, the infant mortality rate remains high (52.4 per 1,000 live births). Health challenges include below-average obesity rates and diseases such as malaria and polio. The U.S. President’s Malaria Initiative aims to reduce malaria cases, and the CDC recommends polio vaccination for travelers. Addressing these issues is crucial for improving living conditions in Côte d'Ivoire.

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged Ivorians, enabling them to achieve self-reliance and pursue prosperity. By providing access to quality job opportunities, we aim to elevate their potential and improve their lives.

Sustainable Impact

We focus on long-term solutions that go beyond quick fixes. Our projects are designed to be sustainable and scalable, aiming for mid to long-term benefits that uplift communities.

Local Engagement

Our founder Stéphane and our local team members understand the unique challenges and opportunities in the Ivory Coast. Their insights guide our initiatives to ensure they are culturally sensitive and practically effective.

Transparency

Trust is paramount. We vow to be transparent about our financials, project status, and impact assessments. Your donations will be handled with the utmost responsibility and integrity.

Education & Economic Growth

We believe that education is the cornerstone of economic prosperity. That's why we fund educational programs and vocational training that prepare Ivorians for the opportunities of tomorrow.
